JOURNALING.

Journaling is about releasing hurt pieces of yourself and gaining beneficial awareness, knowledge, habits, and empowerment.

Journaling is a valuable practice to your healing journey. You are moving your thoughts, dreams, desires,needs, burdens... to paper.

By Journaling emotions become real and recognizable. You are allowing space to see these things and say, "This is here. Now, what do i want to do with it?"

MEANING

A journal is an account which you write of your daily activities. A Journals focus on a specific discipline or field of study. Unlike newspapers and magazines, journals are intended for an academic or technical audience, not general readers.

BENEFITS

> Stay organised

> Emotional strength

> Healing ...
